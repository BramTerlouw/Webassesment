Solar panels:

Solar panels are the future for green electricity. This upcoming product is used to collect solarbeams from the sun and convert them into energy. Environment friendly makes it a real eye catcher on the market. The beautifull designs make them blend in well with any house you can find and over time, anually energy expenses are history. On this page we will take you through every aspect of solarpanels so you know what you can expect from them.

Charge controller:

You can see a Charge controller as a regulator. Its job is to deliver power to system loads and the battery bank. When the battery bank is almost fully charged, the controller will taper of the charging current so the required voltage can be maintained. This product cannot be missed for your solar panels to work efficient. Its available widely and it comes in many different sizes and with different functions.

Battery system:

Battery systems are a very important part of solarpanels and its wisely to choose for a good one. A solar battery works by storing the produced energy from the solar pannels so it can be used on a later moment. The battery converts the energy to form that can be used to power your own for an instance. Most of the energy systems are connected to the grid. Excess energy that you produce is fed back to the power grid.

DC power:

Direct current is an electrical current which flows in one direction consistently. Examples of direct current are the current which flows through your flashlight and other products that run on a battery. The advantage to alternating current (AC) is that its relatively cheap to change the voltage of the current. Besides that the loss of energy over long distances is considerably smaller. This is much bigger when we talk about alternating current.

Inverter:

A inverter is a type of converter for electrical energy. It converts the direct current harvested by the solar pannel into an alternating current. This can be fed into a power grid or can be used by yourself. This item is critical for a solar pannel and battery system and it allows the use of alternating current for machines and products. Solar inverters are widely accessible around the world and come in many different sizes and prices.

AC power:

Alternating current is a current which can flow to multipe direction in contrast to Direct current which can flow to only one direction. Alternating current can be used in all kinds of circuits and machines and is opposite to the direct current which can only flow in one direction. Direct current is converted to alternating current in a inverter. Its much more expensive to change te voltage of the current in contrast to direct current. Also there is more energy loss over longer distances.
